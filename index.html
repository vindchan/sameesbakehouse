Meet Sameeksha

The heart and hands behind Samee's Bakehouse.

With over 13 years of baking experience, she stepped away from the professional kitchen to bring high-quality, handcrafted bakes straight to people's homes.

Trained in the kitchens of Pullman Hotels and Magnolia, she played a key role in launching Magnolia Bakery in Hyderabad.

She combines professional expertise with a deeply personal touch and is known for her ability to bring unique flavors together.

Through Samee's Bakehouse, she creates consciously crafted treats that are as thoughtful as they are delicious.
